## Michael

**Michael** is a supporting character and currently the second level [NPC](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/NPCs "NPCs") in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). Formerly known as Johan Lee, he was a [player](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Players "Players") who quickly befriended [Hanna](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Hanna_Yoo "Hanna Yoo") and [Alex](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Alex_Baek "Alex Baek") in [Level Two](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Levels#Level_2 "Levels"). He is also the husband of [Micaela](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Micaela "Micaela") in the outside world.

## Appearance[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMichael%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

As a human and a player in Zero Game, Johan had short, grey hair with small silver eyes set in a masculine face. After losing his right eye to a fire in the outside world, Johan lost his sight. However, one he entered Zero Game, his right eye regained sight and glowed electric blue, due to his Detection skill. Johan wore black robes under a dark blue dress robe and sported a long necklace with a cross. Once Johan died, he was brought back to life as the [Level Two](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Levels#Level_2 "Levels") NPC, Michael. His appearance remains the same, with the exception of his right eye returning back to its original silver. As Lucifer, Michael sports large, black wings and has horns coming out of his head. He has black markings under both of his eyes and swirls that run across his sides and his arms. He wears black gloves and pants, and a black high-collared jacket. He also wears a large black collar that covers his neck to his collarbones.

## Personality[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMichael%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Michael portrays a very calm and stoic appearance. Due to his regrets of being unable to save his wife back in the outside world, he holds tremendous trauma and guilt. As both NPC and a player Michael is very stubborn. As a player, he would refuse to complete Level Two because he wanted to stay by Micaela's side who he suspected was his dead wife. Though he is sometimes stubborn, Michael is very mature and analytical. He makes calculated decisions when battling opponents and is very quick on his feet.

## Background[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMichael%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Back in the outside world, Michael was a firefighter. After meeting Heather Park, he fell in love and proposed to her. However, she would not accept his hand because she had weak legs and feared they would be a hindrance to him. Heather told him to find someone with a better future, but Michael told her to let both of his legs be her wings to fly high into the air. Afterwards, she accepted his hand and they were married.[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Michael#cite_note-Ep24-1) Instead of wearing a physical wedding band, they decided to wear ring tattoos with their heartbeats engraved in between the band. Heather suggested getting tattoos in fear of Michael's ring burning in the heat of a fire. Later on, a large fire erupted inside of their house and due to Heather's handicapped legs, she was unable to run away. Michael hurriedly tried to reach the house, but his equipment failed and thus lost his right eye and Heather to the fire.[[2]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Michael#cite_note-Ep21-2)
