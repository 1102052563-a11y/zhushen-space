## Description[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FPair_Contract%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

[![Image 3: Pair Code](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/8/8e/Pair_Code.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20200318070358)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/8/8e/Pair_Code.png/revision/latest?cb=20200318070358)

This system allows two players to work together as a team. Two players agree to a Pair Contract, which grants them many advantages. The paired players can share stamina as they progress through the levels together, as well as pinpoint each other's location. Pairs can identify each other by the shared barcodes that appear on their wrists once they sign the contract. If one of the paired players dies within the game, their [Life](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life") is transferred to their partner.

## Features[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FPair_Contract%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

### Pair-Change[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FPair_Contract%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

[![Image 4: Pair Switch](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/3/31/Pair_Switch.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/290?cb=20200411034101)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/3/31/Pair_Switch.png/revision/latest?cb=20200411034101)

Paired players are able to switch bodies at any time. When this occurs, their abilities and passive skills are switched along with the bodies. This feature was primarily developed to allow players to deceive NPCs while advancing levels.
