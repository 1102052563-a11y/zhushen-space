## Players

## Description

Players are living beings from the real world who experience so much suffering, reality is no longer bearable. After receiving an [invitation](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Game_Invitation "Game Invitation"), players have the option to enter the survival game to compete for a chance to rebuild their lives. If a player is eliminated in the game, they perish in the real world.

## Entering Zero Game

Immediately upon entering Zero Game, players are granted a Passive Skill as a tool for basic survival. Due to an intrusion system, new players with limited knowledge of the inner workings of Zero Game are at a considerable disadvantage. Those who survive can journey to the [Ability Shop](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Shop "Ability Shop") to purchase Abilities and Items, kept in their Inventory as they progress through [Levels](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Levels "Levels").
