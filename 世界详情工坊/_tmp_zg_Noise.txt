## Noise

**Noise**, or **System** is a supporting character in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). He is main/head first level [NPC](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/NPCs "NPCs") as well as the System of the game, who monitors all gameplay and has the final say in all decisions made by the [Operator](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._X "Mr. X").

## Appearance[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FNoise%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

As an NPC, Noise has lavender hair that ends at his shoulders and electric blue eyes. He has a half-updo tied together with a long, black ribbon. He is often seen wearing a black cape and tie, along with black gloves. He carries a blue [umbrella](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/NPCs#Umbrella "NPCs") around and was also once seen wearing a pageboy hat.[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Noise#cite_note-1)

As System, his facial features remain the same, but his lavender hair is tied into a ponytail and his hair reaches past his waist. He wears a white suit decorated with gold buttons and a short, underneath a short, white cloak. He also wears the same black gloves. His black tie remains the same but is locked beneath a black string that connects the lapels of his suit, along with a detached, floating Zero Game insignia.
