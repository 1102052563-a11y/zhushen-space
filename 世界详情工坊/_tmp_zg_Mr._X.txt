## Mr. X

**Mr. X**is a supporting character and the current [Operator](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Operators "Operators") in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). He is [Hanna Yoo](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Hanna_Yoo "Hanna Yoo")'s father as well as [Loveme](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Loveme "Loveme")'s husband in the outside world.

## Appearance[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMr._X%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

[![Image 2: Ep4 02](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/2/25/Ep4_02.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20200331224400)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/2/25/Ep4_02.png/revision/latest?cb=20200331224400)

Mr. X has a long frame, light brown hair, and golden eyes. As the Operator, he wears a black suit, a white dress shirt, and a black tie, shoes, gloves, and top hat. When he is disguised as the NPC Zero, he wears a white shirt, glasses, and red scarf. The red scarf can also be seen in flashbacks of his time in the outside world.
