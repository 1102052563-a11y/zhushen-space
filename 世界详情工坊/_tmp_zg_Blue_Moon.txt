## Blue Moon

**Blue Moon** is a guild created and led by [Gina Park](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Jay "Jay"). It grew to become the largest and strongest guilds, securing their candidacy in winning Zero Game.

## Description[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FBlue_Moon%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

The Blue Moon Guild was created and led by [Gina Park](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Jay "Jay"), a resourceful, talented player with an uncanny ability to compel others to join her team. Under her leadership, Blue Moon grew to become the largest and strongest Guild, securing their candidacy in winning Zero Game. Out of 188 guild members, 170 of them were under Gina's [Mind Control](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mind_Control "Mind Control") ability.[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Moon#cite_note-Episode_34-1) Three months prior to the current timeline, [Elliot Baek](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Elliot_Baek "Elliot Baek")[pair-switched](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Pair_Contract "Pair Contract") with [Alex Baek](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Alex_Baek "Alex Baek") and annihilated the entire guild for unknown reasons, sparing only those who were not under Gina's influence.[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Moon#cite_note-Episode_34-1)
