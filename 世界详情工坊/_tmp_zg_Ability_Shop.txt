## Description[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FAbility_Shop%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

The Ability Shop sells abilities to players in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). It closes at 6pm, exactly when the sun goes down. It is run by [Loveme](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Loveme "Loveme"), the fourth-ranked player.[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Shop#cite_note-1)[Mr. X](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._X "Mr. X") has also been seen working part-time at the Ability Shop as 'Zero'.[[2]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Shop#cite_note-2) Even when the Ability Shop closes, there is still an automatic selling machine present.[[3]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Shop#cite_note-3)
