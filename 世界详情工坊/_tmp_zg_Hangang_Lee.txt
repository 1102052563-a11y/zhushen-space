## Hangang Lee

**Hangang Lee** (이한강, _Lee Han-Gang_) is a supporting character and a player in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). He is the Guild Master and Founder of his guild, [Blue Ocean](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Ocean "Blue Ocean").

## Appearance[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Hangang has Purple-Blue hair, with Cyan highlights at the ends and Purple-Blueish eyes. He is introduced and mostly seen in a dark-blue hoodie with his upper body slightly exposed.

## Personality[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Hangang is, as described by [Jasmine Ji](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Jasmine_Ji "Jasmine Ji"), a coward who is also compassionate and generous. He helps out people who are in need, so much so that it had caused him major injuries[[1]](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Hangang_Lee#cite_note-1).

## Background[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

When Hangang was a child, someone saved him from being hit by a car. The female who helped him hugged him and said it was okay, despite it being Hangang's fault. From that incident onwards, Hangang decided to help those who were in danger; lend a hand to those who needed it. However, it was hard enough to just look after himself, and helping someone was never easy.

From helping someone, he lost his hand. Before entering [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"), Hangang deeply regretted helping that person, even wishing that he had just ignored it and looked the other way. Because of that, he was invited and joined Zero Game. After joining Zero Game, he no longer regretted helping them.

Hangang used to love swimming, but couldn't do that after losing his arm.

After joining [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"), he helped many people in need and/or saved them from death or injuries. These people came together, and thus Blue Ocean was formed.

## Powers and Abilities[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

### Confinement[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D5&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Hangang's passive, Confinement, can bind any person he chooses for 3 minutes. The cooldown time is 2 hours. Blue chains appear to wrap around the target when used.

### Water Master[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHangang_Lee%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D6&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Hangang's ability, Water Master, is a SSS rank ability which allows Hangang to (assumption) manipulate water as he wishes. He was seen using the ability to separate an immense amount of water in half to walk through it. The cooldown is unknown.
