## 剧情

**【作品来源】**
The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass is Nintendo EAD's 2007 Nintendo DS action-adventure game, directed by Daiki Iwamoto and produced by Eiji Aonuma. It is a direct sequel to The Wind Waker, following the same Link and Tetra after their voyage to find a new land beyond the flooded Hyrule of the Great Sea. Its small islands, bright cel-shaded water, stylus-drawn sea routes, and comic captain do not make it a harmless holiday. The Ocean King Temple consumes a visitor's life, the Ghost Ship leaves victims as stone, and Bellum literally feeds on life force. The adventure ends before Spirit Tracks: Link and Tetra return to Tetra's pirate ship, while Linebeck receives his ship back and the Ocean King departs in whale form.

**【世界定位】**
This is an oceanic rescue story about navigation, time pressure, and a false treasure hunt. Link crosses the Great Sea to save Tetra after the Ghost Ship takes her. Ciela, a fairy with missing memories; Linebeck, a cowardly treasure hunter; and Oshus, an apparently harmless island elder, form the expedition. The central question is not who can defeat every guardian by force: Bellum's curse makes repeated intrusion into the Ocean King Temple fatal until Link obtains the Phantom Hourglass, and Bellum himself cannot be properly killed until three pure metals become the Phantom Sword.

**【世界观 · 力量体系】**
The Great Sea is the post-Wind Waker ocean. Islands are independent settlements and dungeon sites, connected by routes on the S.S. Linebeck rather than roads. A sea chart is operational intelligence: Link draws a course, changes it when reefs or enemies appear, fires the ship's cannon, and later uses salvage and the crane. A voyage can be interrupted by sea monsters, patrols, drifting hazards, or a whirlpool; navigation knowledge is therefore a genuine resource rather than decorative map lore.

Most residents are ordinary sailors, shopkeepers, islanders, Gorons, or travellers. Link is an unusually capable human hero, armed with sword, shield, bombs, boomerang, bow, grappling hook, shovel, hammer, and eventually the Phantom Sword. His endurance is represented by hearts; fairies and potions can restore him, but death or petrification is never a casual reset inside the fiction. Combat remains close-range and tactical: arrows trigger distant switches, bombs break walls, the boomerang stuns and carries fire, and the grappling hook links posts or steals enemy shields. No islander gains Link's arsenal merely because a contracter arrives.

The Ocean King Temple is governed by a specific curse. Its lower floors contain Phantoms, enormous armoured guards that ordinary weapons cannot harm. Safe zones protect Link from both Phantoms and the drain, while the Phantom Hourglass contains Sands of Hours that halt the drain only for a limited period. Leaving the temple restores its rooms but Link retains acquired charts, keys, shortcuts, and item-enabled routes. Later, the Phantom Sword can kill Phantoms; before it is forged, stealth, distractions, pots, switches, and route planning are mandatory. This creates a hard distinction between an infiltration mission and a monster hunt.

Three Spirits maintain the Ocean King's protective order: Leaf of Power, Neri of Wisdom, and Ciela of Courage. Bellum attacked Ciela and consumed part of her power; Oshus divided her soul, leaving Ciela without memory and the other half confined as the Spirit of Courage. Bellum survives by devouring life, hides beneath the temple, controls the Ghost Ship, and turns Tetra to stone. It is not simply a large sea monster that can be beaten with the regular sword. The three pure metals, Crimsonine, Azurine, and Aquanine, must be brought to Zauz so he can forge the Phantom Sword. That blade is the condition that lets Link destroy Bellum and free the petrified Tetra.

The game has no canonical power levels, so the mapping follows observed scale. Ordinary islanders, sailors, and new pirates are first tier; trained guards, minor monsters, and equipped adventurers are second tier; dungeon guardians such as Octorok formations, Stalfos captains, and hostile sea-creatures are third tier; Link with the full conventional kit and major dungeon bosses are fourth tier; the Ocean King Temple's Phantom network and the Ghost Ship's command structure are fifth tier area threats; Bellum at the temple core or while possessing Linebeck is the local sixth-tier apex. The Ocean King Oshus is an ancient divine protector and must be treated as a sixth-tier-plus existence whose help is rule-bound, not a summonable combat reward. 乐园阶位映射：岛民与水手≈一阶；普通怪物和武装守卫≈二阶；精英怪物与岛屿守卫≈三阶；持完整常规装备的林克和各神殿首领≈四阶；幽灵、幽灵船与海王神殿防卫体系≈五阶；贝拉姆本体≈六阶；海王神奥修斯为超出常规任务者。 Bellum can only be defeated conditionally with the Phantom Sword and the completed Spirit chain; information and preparation come before confrontation.

**【地理 · 舞台】**
Mercay Island is the landing point after Link falls from Tetra's ship. Oshus's house and the entrance to the Ocean King Temple make it both a refuge and the first recurring danger. The Temple's upper and lower levels yield sea charts for new quadrants: the Northwest, Southwest, Northeast, and Southeast Seas. Each return is meaningful because a new tool opens a shortcut, not because the same floor becomes a random battlefield.

Linebeck's ship is the mobile base. It begins as a treasure hunter's paddle steamer and gains cannon, salvage arm, and other sea functions. Cannon Island supplies artillery; Molida Island has a tavern, a hidden cave, and the first sea chart lead; Isle of Ember holds the Fire Temple; Cannon Island and the surrounding waters teach naval danger. Goron Island hosts the Goron Temple and tests strength and trade. Isle of Frost leads toward ice, while the Isle of Ruins and the Mutoh's Temple region contain older labyrinthine culture. Bannan Island, the Maze Island area, and smaller unnamed islets support treasure maps, ship parts, and side rescues, but should not be mistaken for kingdom-scale states.

The Ghost Ship appears through sudden fog and cannot be pursued by ordinary sailing. It is a moving prison where Tetra lies petrified. The final route returns to the Ocean King Temple's deepest chamber, then to the Ghost Ship for Bellum's possession of Linebeck. After Bellum falls, the sea looks unchanged to Tetra's crew because only about ten minutes have passed in their world; the empty hourglass and Linebeck's ship on the horizon leave the voyage deliberately uncanny.

**【世界剧情线】**
After The Wind Waker, Link and Tetra sail with her pirate crew in search of new land. A fog-bank reveals the Ghost Ship. Tetra leaps aboard despite Link's warning, screams, and disappears. Link tries to follow, falls into the sea, and wakes on Mercay Island. This opening defines the real emergency: Tetra is not merely missing at sea but taken by a vessel that ignores normal routes. Ciela finds Link and brings him to her adoptive grandfather Oshus, who knows enough to direct him toward the Ocean King Temple.

At first Linebeck joins only because he imagines the Ghost Ship holds treasure. He refuses heroic language, argues over risk, and keeps his ship as his most valued possession. Yet he sails Link from Mercay after Link enters the temple and obtains the first sea chart. The temple initially drains Link's life while its Phantoms patrol. Link must observe their cones of vision, hide in purple safe zones, run paths under time pressure, and retrieve charts rather than kill guards. This early return establishes that a contracter can help with scouting, marked routes, or a diversion, but cannot truthfully promise to clear Phantoms with ordinary steel.

The first regional objective is to release the Spirit of Power. Link follows clues through the Isle of Ember and the Fire Temple, using a boomerang path to guide fire and solve switches, then reaches the Temple of Wind. There he confronts the wind guardian and frees Leaf. The sea opens further, but the next temple is not merely “more of the same”: the Spirit of Wisdom's trail leads to a different island culture and puzzle logic, including the use of bombs, arrows, and sea travel upgrades. Each liberated Spirit restores part of the protective order that Bellum has weakened.

The Ocean King Temple repeatedly changes Link's priorities. A deeper chart lets him sail to a new sea, but returning through the temple consumes the remaining time in the hourglass. Link learns to draw notes on his map, identify locked doors that a later item will open, and use a newly gained tool to skip long exposed corridors. The reset of the temple is diegetic pressure: the architecture remains hostile while knowledge survives. A player who fights every monster wastes the very resource the plot says is scarce.

The Spirit of Wisdom route supplies more of the ship's operational capacity and leads Link across islands with their own problems, including the Gorons. Goron Island's residents are not generic miners: they demand proof of strength, knowledge of their customs, and access to their temple. The Goron Temple turns weight, rolling hazards, and heavy enemies into the puzzle language. Link's gains, including strength-related access and tools, allow him to re-read old rooms in the Ocean King Temple. Side voyages may uncover ship parts, treasure maps, and rescued sailors; these enrich Linebeck's vessel but never substitute for the three spirits or the pure metals.

The third spirit is the decisive emotional reveal. Link reaches the Spirit of Courage and Ciela reacts to her resemblance. Oshus admits that Ciela is not simply an orphan fairy: Bellum attacked her, and Oshus split her soul so that a surviving half could remain hidden. Reuniting Ciela with the Spirit of Courage restores her identity and clarifies that the old man is the Ocean King in a human disguise. Oshus had imprisoned Bellum under his temple after fighting it, but Bellum's ability to consume life and corrupt the temple's systems kept the prison from being a final solution.

With all three Spirits present, Link can find the Ghost Ship. The rescue looks successful only for a moment: Tetra's body is motionless and petrified. Oshus explains that her restoration requires Bellum's death, and Bellum cannot be killed by Link's existing blade. The next quest is technical and mythic rather than a search for a random superweapon. Link must acquire the three pure metals, Crimsonine, Azurine, and Aquanine, each guarded by separate exploration barriers, then take them to the smith Zauz. Zauz forges the Phantom Sword, a weapon specifically capable of destroying Phantoms and harming Bellum.

The metal quest expands the sea without erasing local stakes. Link explores the Isle of Frost and ice-bound routes, negotiates or proves himself among the Gorons, travels through ruins whose switches and symbols require map notes, and returns to old routes with the bow, grappling hook, hammer, shovel, or bombs. A contracter can assist by carrying a map clue, keeping a sea lane clear, rescuing a stranded crew, or opening a secondary route. They cannot replace Link in receiving the Phantom Sword or claim an invented fourth metal; the weapon's three named components and Zauz's craft are the canonical bottleneck.

Once the Phantom Sword is complete, Link can finally kill Phantoms rather than merely evade them. This changes the deepest Ocean King Temple from a pure stealth siege into a dangerous assault, but it does not remove the hourglass timer or Bellum's environmental control. Link descends to Bellum's core and defeats the creature. He is transported back to Linebeck's ship and reunited with a revived Tetra, only for Bellum to seize her again and retreat to the Ghost Ship. The apparent victory was incomplete because Bellum still has a vessel and a final host strategy.

Link uses Linebeck's cannon to destroy the Ghost Ship's outer threat. Bellum then possesses Linebeck, forcing Link to fight the captain he has travelled with. This is not proof that Linebeck was secretly malicious: Bellum hijacks him after the ship is destroyed. Link must damage Bellum while avoiding a lethal finish on the possessed captain. Bellum is killed, Linebeck is freed, and Tetra is rescued. The S.S. Linebeck is lost in the battle, but Oshus grants Linebeck one wish. Having learned loyalty, Linebeck gives up the chance for treasure and asks for his ship restored.

Oshus returns to his white-whale form and leaves with Leaf, Neri, and Ciela. Link and Tetra reappear on Tetra's pirate ship. Her crew believes only ten minutes have passed and treats the whole journey as a strange dream. Link holds the now-empty Phantom Hourglass and sees Linebeck's restored ship far away, confirming the adventure was real. The ending preserves the consequence of Bellum's defeat without pretending that the Great Sea became a fully mapped, permanently safe empire.

**【势力图谱】**
- **海王与三精灵**：由**奥修斯**、**叶子**、**纳莉**、**西艾拉**构成的守护一方，控制神殿的保护规则却受贝拉姆污染牵制。
- **贝拉姆与幽灵船**：由**贝拉姆**操纵，靠雾、石化与附身捕猎生命；没有稳定人类政权，优势是突袭和神殿深层地利。
- **泰特拉海盗团**：由**泰特拉**率领，拥有普通船员和航海经验；故事开端无法进入幽灵船，终局也不是主力军。
- **莱因巴克的航船**：由**莱因巴克**和**林克**实际使用，是探索、补给和海战平台；它不是海王军舰，失去后需要奥修斯的愿望才恢复。
- **岛屿社群与戈隆人**：各自管理贸易、试炼和生活空间，能提供情报、物资或通行条件，不服从统一王权。

**【贵重物品】**
- **梦幻沙漏Phantom Hourglass**：盛放Sands of Hours，在海王神殿内暂时阻止生命流失；沙尽则保护失效，终局后变为空壳。
- **幻影之剑Phantom Sword**：由**佐兹**用Crimsonine、Azurine、Aquanine锻造，能杀死幽灵并伤害贝拉姆；不能在未收齐三矿前提前出现。
- **海图Sea Charts**：藏于海王神殿，决定可航行海域；它们是地理权限，不是普通藏宝图。
- **S.S. Linebeck**：莱因巴克的桨轮船，可绘航线、装船炮、打捞；最终毁于战斗后才由愿望恢复。
- **回旋镖、抓钩、弓、锤、炸弹**：林克的解谜与战斗工具，各对应火源、远程开关、连接、重击或爆破，不能概括成虚构法术。
- **三种纯金属**：赤红矿、蔚蓝矿、海蓝矿是锻造幻影之剑的必要材料，剧情价值在于共同构成对贝拉姆的唯一有效武器。

**【隐藏剧情 · 伏笔】**
Oshus's harmless old-man appearance conceals the Ocean King because Bellum has already injured the protective order. Ciela's missing memory is not amnesia for comedy: she is the separated half of the Spirit of Courage, split after Bellum's attack. The Temple's life drain tells the player before the reveal that the prison has been compromised. Linebeck's treasure obsession also foreshadows the ending reversal: when granted one wish, he asks for his ship rather than imaginary gold, proving he has become more than a guide hired for profit. Bellum's first defeat is incomplete because a life-devouring entity can retreat to the Ghost Ship and seize a host; do not end the story at the core battle.

**【大事记时间线】**
Wind Waker aftermath → Link and Tetra sail the Great Sea seeking new land.  
Ghost Ship incident → Tetra boards the fog-bound ship; Link falls and reaches Mercay Island.  
First temple descent → Ciela and Oshus direct Link to the Ocean King Temple; Linebeck joins for treasure.  
Three Spirit quest → Link frees Leaf, Neri, and the Spirit of Courage while collecting sea charts.  
Identity reveal → Oshus explains Ciela is part of Courage and reveals himself as the Ocean King.  
Ghost Ship rescue → Link finds petrified Tetra and learns Bellum requires the Phantom Sword to defeat.  
Forge quest → Link obtains Crimsonine, Azurine, Aquanine; Zauz makes the Phantom Sword.  
Final battles → Link defeats Bellum in the temple, destroys the Ghost Ship, frees possessed Linebeck, and kills Bellum.  
Return → Oshus restores Linebeck's ship; Link and Tetra return after only ten minutes have passed for the pirate crew.

**【叙事基调 · 雷区】**
Keep the bright maritime humour together with real clock pressure and eerie disappearances. Do not treat the Ocean King Temple as a generic loot dungeon: its route, safe zones, timer, and invulnerable Phantoms are the story's central rule. Do not call Linebeck a villain because Bellum possesses him. Do not let Tetra freely fight while she is petrified. The earliest valid entry is the Ghost Ship's appearance before Tetra boards, or Link's arrival on Mercay Island after the fall.

## 阶位切入点

> 阶位↔原作强度：岛民与水手≈一阶；普通怪物和护卫≈二阶；精英怪物与岛屿守卫≈三阶；全装备林克与神殿首领≈四阶；幽灵、幽灵船、海王神殿防卫体系≈五阶；贝拉姆本体≈六阶。**贝拉姆**与**奥修斯**均为顶点相关存在，低阶优先调查海图、路线和封印条件；击杀贝拉姆必须以幻影之剑为前提。

**一阶（梅尔卡岛 · 幽灵船失踪）**

切入身份/时点：契约者作为泰特拉海盗团见习水手或梅尔卡岛渔民，在幽灵船起雾、泰特拉将要登船时切入。

初始事件：海面传来尖叫，**泰特拉**跳向幽灵船；契约者可拦住恐慌船员、抛绳救坠海的**林克**，或追踪雾的方向，但只能选一项优先完成。

开场白建议：「海图上本该空白的水域浮出一艘破船，船帆像湿透的葬布。泰特拉已经踩上栏杆，下一瞬雾里传来她的喊声。甲板倾斜时，一只手抓住你的腕骨，林克正要跃过去，而船员们已经开始拔锚逃命。」

关键NPC立场：**泰特拉**急于查明危险；**林克**只会先救她；**西艾拉**在梅尔卡岛愿意帮助陌生求生者；**奥修斯**先观察来客是否会破坏神殿秩序；**莱因巴克**只对宝藏传闻有兴趣。

主线钩子/支线：介入“Ghost Ship incident”；支线为救起落水者、记录雾的出现时间、替西艾拉寻找梅尔卡岛失踪者线索。

危险度/规避：中。幽灵船不可登；不要把普通船驶入浓雾深处，落海后优先寻找梅尔卡岛海岸。

任务方向/奖励：救援与取证；奖励取向为海盗团信任、梅尔卡岛补给、第一手航海情报。

**二阶（海王神殿上层 · 沙漏前的潜行）**

切入身份/时点：契约者以梅尔卡岛探索者或莱因巴克船上的临时甲板员身份，在林克首次进入海王神殿、尚未取得梦幻沙漏时切入。

初始事件：一名误入神殿的岛民被幽灵巡逻逼进死角，**林克**必须继续寻找海图；契约者要制造声响引开幽灵，还是带岛民退回入口，会影响是否暴露林克的路线。

开场白建议：「紫色安全区只够两人并肩站立，外面的铁甲幽灵每走一步，胸口的灯便扫过整条走廊。你的手背开始发冷，仿佛生命正从皮肤下被抽走。林克在墙角画下路线，轻声示意：下一扇门里有海图，但也没有第二个藏身处。」

关键NPC立场：**林克**优先取得海图；**西艾拉**能提示方向却无法击倒幽灵；**奥修斯**不会直接破坏自己的试炼；**莱因巴克**在船上等待结果，不会为陌生人进入神殿。

主线钩子/支线：钩子是取得第一海图；支线为标记安全区、护送岛民离开、找出能缩短下次路线的开关。

危险度/规避：高。幽灵不可被常规武器杀伤，生命流失尚未被沙漏抵消；不可追逐战利品而离开安全区。

任务方向/奖励：带出路线笔记或救出误入者；奖励取向为神殿地图知识、基础恢复物和莱因巴克的航行许可。

**三阶（火与风之岛链 · 解放力量精灵）**

切入身份/时点：契约者作为岛屿信使、船炮观察员或林克的外围探险者，在火焰神殿至风之神殿、寻找**叶子**期间切入。

初始事件：火焰神殿的门锁要求把火种用回旋镖送过沟壑，怪物已包围一名带路的岛民；救人会耗掉火种窗口，抢先进门则可能失去通往风之神殿的口述线索。

开场白建议：「熔岩把石桥切成几段，回旋镖拖着一线火光绕过柱子。岛民缩在墙后，风声却从深处带来另一个警告：叶子的牢笼正在关闭。林克看向你，没有命令，只把一枚炸弹递来，像是在问你愿不愿意把下一步画在同一张地图上。」

关键NPC立场：**林克**要释放**叶子**；**叶子**被囚无法直接援助；**西艾拉**帮助辨认精灵气息；**莱因巴克**会在危险海域催促返航；神殿守卫只按机关与领地攻击。

主线钩子/支线：钩子是力量精灵解放；支线为修复岛间信号、护送火种携带者、清理妨碍船炮靠岸的海怪。

危险度/规避：高。火焰与风机关可致命；不要把精灵牢笼当作可交易物，避开神殿首领正面战。

任务方向/奖励：完成护送、提供正确地图标记；奖励取向为海图权限、耐热补给、岛屿人脉。

**四阶（戈隆与纯金属 · 造剑前夜）**

切入身份/时点：契约者作为戈隆岛试炼参与者、考古助手或S.S. Linebeck护航者，在三精灵已齐、林克寻找三种纯金属并拜访**佐兹**时切入。

初始事件：戈隆岛的试炼通道因塌方断开，运送给**佐兹**的矿石线索会被潮水冲走；契约者可协助**林克**通过力量试炼，或救回被困的戈隆商人，两者都将改变矿石取得的顺序。

开场白建议：「戈隆岛的石门在你面前轰然合拢，门上只留下一句刻痕：力量不是抢来的。远处的海潮正漫过装着矿石记录的箱子，林克已把抓钩甩向崖壁。佐兹在岛另一端等材料，而幽灵船的雾正沿着海平线逼近。」

关键NPC立场：**佐兹**只接受真正的三种纯金属；**林克**必须完成锻剑链；**西艾拉**恢复记忆后会坚持阻止贝拉姆；**莱因巴克**担心船和宝藏；戈隆居民重视本岛规则与互助。

主线钩子/支线：钩子是Crimsonine、Azurine、Aquanine与幻影之剑；支线为戈隆试炼、护送矿石情报、用抓钩打开旧遗迹的补给室。

危险度/规避：高。守卫和遗迹机关约四阶；不要伪造第四种矿石或提前持有幻影之剑，避免幽灵船雾区。

任务方向/奖励：确保一条金属线索或协助锻造前准备；奖励取向为戈隆交易资格、稀有矿材、神殿通行与装备维护。

**五阶（幽灵船与神殿深层 · 幽灵可杀）**

切入身份/时点：契约者以幽灵船侦察员、神殿深层路线员或莱因巴克的炮手身份，在林克持幻影之剑后进入神殿底层、寻找石化的**泰特拉**时切入。

初始事件：深层幽灵封死通往海图的走廊，**林克**能用幻影之剑开路，但一名被石化前留下的船员仍困在侧室；契约者必须决定守住安全区、引开幽灵，或护送记录返回船上。

开场白建议：「幻影之剑第一次刺穿铁甲时，神殿的钟声像从海底传来。你以为计时终于结束，却看见沙漏里的光已经只剩一线。泰特拉就在更深的幽灵船里，而每一扇新门后都可能是贝拉姆留下的假出口。」

关键NPC立场：**林克**以幻影之剑处理幽灵；**泰特拉**已石化不能行动；**贝拉姆**故意用幽灵船和假线索拖延；**莱因巴克**愿意靠近危险海域但怕失去船；**奥修斯**要求先完成正确武器和精灵条件。

主线钩子/支线：钩子是登幽灵船、确认泰特拉状态与进入底层；支线为记录捷径、救出受困船员、用船炮清理航线障碍。

危险度/规避：极高。幽灵与神殿体系是五阶压力；沙漏时间必须保留给主路线，未经幻影之剑不可挑战巡逻幽灵。

任务方向/奖励：保障泰特拉定位、保留撤离路线；奖励取向为深层海图、幽灵剑术观察、莱因巴克船队信用。

**六阶（贝拉姆终局 · 幻影之剑条件胜利）**

切入身份/时点：契约者以海王神殿终局支援者、灵魂记录保全员或S.S. Linebeck最后炮手身份，在贝拉姆核心战到其附身莱因巴克期间切入。

初始事件：**贝拉姆**在核心败退后又夺走**泰特拉**，幽灵船即将离开；契约者要操作船炮击毁外壳、护送**西艾拉**脱离坍塌通道，或保存海王神殿的记录，三项无法同时完成。

开场白建议：「海王神殿深处的触手缩回黑暗，所有人都以为泰特拉能醒来。下一秒，幽灵船的雾从甲板上卷走她，莱因巴克的船炮被迫对准同伴。林克握紧幻影之剑，你却听见海王的声音提醒：能伤到贝拉姆，不等于能在错误的地方杀死它。」

关键NPC立场：**贝拉姆**以吞噬与附身求生；**林克**是唯一能以幻影之剑终结它的人；**莱因巴克**被附身，必须被救而非处决；**泰特拉**是被夺回的人质；**奥修斯**在规则完成后才可实现愿望。

主线钩子/支线：钩子是毁灭幽灵船、解除莱因巴克附身、杀死贝拉姆；支线为稳定炮位、回收沙漏残片、协助终局后岛民确认航线安全。

危险度/规避：贴近顶点。**贝拉姆**为六阶顶点，胜利条件是幻影之剑、先前精灵链与正确目标识别；存在与情报优先，不能以其“虚弱”解释成零战力。

任务方向/奖励：确保条件链不被打断、救回泰特拉和莱因巴克；奖励取向为海王认可、稀有航海图、救援人脉与终局见证，非无限使用的神力。
