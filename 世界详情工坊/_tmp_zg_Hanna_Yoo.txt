## Hanna Yoo

Overview

**Hanna Yoo** (유한라, _Yoo Hanra_) is the main protagonist and is currently the first ranking [player](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Players "Players") in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). She befriends [Alex Baek](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Alex_Baek "Alex Baek") immediately after entering the game and [pair-shares](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Pair_Contract "Pair Contract") with him. She is also the daughter of [Mr. X](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._X "Mr. X") and [Loveme](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Loveme "Loveme") in the real world.

Hanna has long, light brown hair typically styled in a tieback braid or ponytail. Her gold, amber eyes are framed with thick lashes, set in a slim, feminine face. When she is not in gameplay attire, Hanna usually dons a yellow sweater or cardigan, a white shirt, and a black skirt and shoes.

## Personality[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHanna_Yoo%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

[![Image 2: Hanna 06](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/2/2e/Hanna_06.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/170?cb=20200413062849)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/zero-game/images/2/2e/Hanna_06.png/revision/latest?cb=20200413062849)

Though initially depressed by her empty life in the real world, Hanna adopted an optimistic and somewhat naive attitude after entering Zero Game. Her determination to win the survival game keeps her diligent, focused, and driven. Having dealt with bullies in the past, Hanna is very sharp and observant of others. Her quick wit and analytical skills give Hanna the necessary survival instinct to safely progress through [levels](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Levels "Levels") and deal with deceitful, manipulative opponents. Despite this, Hanna refuses to compromise her morals to achieve her goal. Unlike most players, Hanna remains benevolent and respectful of her competitors and enemies.
