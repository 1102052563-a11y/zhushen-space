## Loveme

**Loveme** (러브미, _Leobeumi_) is a supporting characer and currently the fourth ranking [player](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Players "Players") in [Zero Game](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Zero_Game "Zero Game"). She was the former Operator and the owner of the [Ability Shop](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Shop "Ability Shop"). She is also [Hanna Yoo](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Hanna_Yoo "Hanna Yoo")'s deceased mother and [Mr. X](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._X "Mr. X")'s wife in the outside world.

## Appearance[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fzero-game.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FLoveme%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")]

Loveme has a yellow-to-pink ombre hair color, and golden eyes, similar to [Mr. X](https://zero-game.fandom.com/wiki/Mr._X "Mr. X") and Hanna Yoo. She is usually seen with her hair tied back into a high ponytail while wearing pink heart sunglasses. She wears a dark red trench coat with a black tie topped with a red and yellow broken heart.

As a former operator, Loveme wears the standard operator attire, with a black trench coat and a black top hat. She is also often seen with a green and yellow aura, indicating her powers as an operator.
